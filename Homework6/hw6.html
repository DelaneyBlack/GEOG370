Homework 7: Census data choropleth

Delaney Black

This choropleth depicts the rate of poverty in Georgia by county as a percent of the county's total population. A darker green tone represents a higher rate of poverty. Forsyth County has the lowest rate of poverty in the state at 4.5%, while Calhoun County has the highet rate at 35.9%.This data can be used to determine allocation of relief & welfare resources at the state level or can be compared to other variables such as average income for a deeper analysis. Data was retrieved from the United States Census Bureau and represents the rates of poverty in 2019.
